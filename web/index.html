How to use

  1. Click 'create new sector'
  2. Click once to select top/left corner
  3. Move mouse to visualize the sector
  4. Click once to select bottom/right corner
  5. If the sector is fine, click 'YES'
  6. Click 'Get average color' if no car is inside the sector
  7. Create as many sectors as you want and click 'play'
